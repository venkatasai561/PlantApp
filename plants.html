Here are the some rare Plants

Corpse Flower (Amorphophallus titanum)
The Corpse Flower (Amorphophallus titanum) is renowned for its large size and foul odor attracting pollinators.

Rafflesia arnoldii Rafflesia arnoldii, the largest flower in the world, has a strong, unpleasant odor of decaying flesh to lure its pollinators. Its blooming is rare and short-lived.

Encephalartos woodii (Wood's Cycad) Encephalartos woodii, also known as Wood's Cycad, is an extinct-in-the-wild species, notable for having no known female plants, thus being unable to reproduce naturally.

Ghost Orchid (Dendrophylax lindenii) The Ghost Orchid (Dendrophylax lindenii) is a rare, leafless orchid known for its elusive nature and fragrant, ghostly white flowers.
